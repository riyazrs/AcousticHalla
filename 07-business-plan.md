# 07 Business Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
AcousticHalla provides B2B cognitive enrichment hardware and analytics software designed to eradicate captivity-induced stress in large mammals. By combining bio-acoustic feedback (a giant interactive piano interface) with novel gustatory rewards (e.g., lime-infused treats), we stimulate neuroplasticity in ways traditional static toys cannot. 

## 2. Market Analysis
- **TAM:** The global zoo, aquarium, and wildlife conservation market is valued at ~$2.4 Billion. 
- **Urgency:** Rising public scrutiny over animal welfare and stricter government compliance laws force institutions to modernize their enrichment programs. 
- **Target Audience:** Top 50 global zoological parks and well-funded private sanctuaries.

## 3. Go-to-Market & Marketing Campaign
**Campaign Name:** *"Hear the Wild"*
- **Strategy:** Direct B2B enterprise sales targeting Chief Veterinary Officers and Head Zoologists. 
- **Content:** Publish peer-reviewed pilot studies demonstrating a 40%+ drop in animal cortisol levels when using AcousticHalla pods. 
- **Events:** Booths at the World Association of Zoos and Aquariums (WAZA) Annual Conference.
- **Digital:** High-end documentary-style videos showing a Giant Panda interacting with the pod, highlighting the contrast between the majestic animal and the sleek, modern hardware. 

## 4. Financial Projections
- **Revenue Model:** Hardware setup fee ($15,000 per 3-pod enclosure) + SaaS subscription for behavioral analytics ($1,500/month/enclosure).
- **Year 1 Target:** Secure 10 pilot parks (30 pods deployed) = $150k hardware revenue + $180k ARR. 
- **Year 3 Target:** 150 parks = $2.7M ARR.

## 5. Funding Requirements
Seeking **$1.5M Seed Funding** to scale hardware manufacturing, finalize the proprietary edge-AI engagement algorithm, and expand the direct sales team. Pitching to impact-focused funds and prominent accelerators like YCombinator and TechStars.
