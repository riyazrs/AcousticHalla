# 04 Solution Design

**Solution Name:** AcousticHalla
**Format:** Hybrid (Hardware + AI-Driven Software Service)
**Provider:** A B2B Conservation-Tech Startup 

**The Offering: "The Harmonic Enrichment Pod"**
AcousticHalla designs and manufactures an interactive, weather-proof cognitive enrichment system for large mammals. The hardware consists of ruggedized, oversized capacitive-touch "keys" (mimicking a piano) integrated with an automated, scent-infused reward dispenser (capable of dispensing novel tastes like lime or other citrus to trigger olfactory novelty). 

When the animal interacts with the keys, the system produces responsive, mathematically harmonious sounds and dispenses treats. 

**The Software (SaaS Component):**
The physical pod is equipped with biometric and computer-vision sensors. An AI backend tracks the animal's interaction patterns, rhythm complexity, and dwell time. It autonomously adjusts the musical scale and reward frequency to optimize the animal's engagement, actively combatting habituation. The data is piped into a cloud dashboard for zoologists, providing real-time behavioral analytics, stress-reduction metrics, and automated compliance reports for animal welfare regulators.
